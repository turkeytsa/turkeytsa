2017 Events

Photographic Technology

This event requires participants to reflect a message related to the theme of the year “Humor” through photographic and imaging technology processes. Semifinalists then prepare a single image for an on-site prompt on a graphic editing software. Özge Özkaymak will represent TSA Turkey in this event with her expressive photos.

Software Development

Attendees are expected to develop, apply, test and document software that would be educationally or socially valuable. Ömer Erdinç Yağmurlu and Nihan Şentürk are developing software through algorithms, problem-solving principles and teamwork from which teachers and students would benefit from.

Webmaster

Participants of this event develop a website that would introduce the participant’s school and its TSA Chapter. Lara Turgut, Ömer Erdinç Yağmurlu and Kemal Fidancı aim to introduce Istanbul High School and TSA Turkey Chapter thoroughly with their creatively designed website.

Technology Problem Solving

Attendees develop a solution to a problem given on site with their skills in problem solving. …. … will represent TSA Turkey in this event.

Technology Bowl

Participants take a test regarding to TSA and concepts of technology content standards. After this first round, semifinalists take part in question/response, head-to-head team competition. .. … .. will represent TSA Turkey in this event in June.

System Control Technology

Participating teams receive a problem usually from an industrial setting, and after analyzing the problem they develop a computer-controlled model solution through programming. Deniz Gökay Özkır, Firdevs Eren and …. will demonstrate their problem-solving skills in June at the national conference.

Future Technolgoy Teacher

Participants investigate and document technology education preparation programs in colleges or universities and design a lesson plan. Firdevs Eren will represent TSA Turkey in this event.

Extemporaneous Speech

Attendees hold a spontaneous presentation about the topic written on the card they have thrown. The subject is related to technology or a TSA topic. ….. will take part in this on site event.

Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM)

Participants are required to design and fabricate a product that would promote the current conference city and/or state. Deniz Gökay Özkır and Kemal Fidancı will showcase Orlando, Florida with their product.

Architectural Design

Attendees design a computer-generated model of an architectural design related to the current year’s challenge and construct a physical product as well. Pınar Bulut and Kemal Fidancı are designing a convenient shipping container that would fit LEED Standards.

Music Production

Ömer Faruk Bildik, Arda Nacar and Emine Özen attends to this event with an energizing song that would be presented during TSA opening or closing ceremonies.

Scientific Visualization (SciVis)

Attendees create processes on 2D/3D graphic tools to illustrate a STEM concept. Deniz Gökay Özkır, Ömer Erdinç Yağmurlu and Ömer Faruk Bildik aim to inform general public about the electric cars with their illustrations.

Essays on Technology

Participants write a research-based essay related to a technology topic with the help of provided sources on site. ….. will represent TSA Turkey for this event.

Prepared Presentation

This event requires participants to prepare an oral presentation using a digital slide deck to a topic assigned on site. Emine Özen and Nihan Şentürk will represent TSA Turkey for this event.

On Demand Video

Participants are expected to write, shoot, and edit a 60-second video on-site during the conference.

Digital Video Production

Participants develop a public service announcement and shoot a video with sound regarding to current year’s theme. Özge Özkaymak is writing, directing and editing the video about “Standing Out from the Crowd” in which members of TSA Turkey are acting.

Fashion Design and Technology

Participants research, design, create a portfolio and a wearable prototype relating to the current year’s theme. Eda Kahraman, Nihan Şentürk, Pınar Bulut, Lara Turgut are designing garments out of recycled materials for this year’s conference.

Engineering Design

This event requires competitors to develop a solution to a grand challenge posted on the national TSA website. In 2017, Deniz Ece Sönmez and Zeynep Duygulu design a solution to enable access to clean water for places in need.

Coding

Participants receive a coding-related design challenge on-site and are expected to develop a software program under limited time. Ömer Erdinç Yağmurlu and Nihan Şentürk will represent TSA Turkey in this event.

Computer Aided Design (CAD), Engineering

Participants use complex graphic tools to develop 3D representations of engineering subjects such as machine part, tool, or manufactured product. Deniz Gökay Özkır and Kemal Fidancı will represent TSA Turkey in this event.

Children’s Stories

Attendees write and illustrate an instructional children’s story that carries a social value. The story with STEM integration must be presented in a physical book. Firdevs Eren, Emine Özen, Lara Turgut and Eda Kahraman are creating a pop-up book for the conference in 2017.

Biotechnology Design

The event requires students to identify a current biotechnology problem related to the theme of the year and develop a solution. Emine Özen, Firdevs Eren, Deniz Ece Sönmez, Zeynep Duygulu document a possible solution to high costs of vaccines for this year’s theme “Vaccines”.
