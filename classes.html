Classes and Courses

Arduino Classes

The students improve themselves in the areas of microcontrollers and embedded systems engineering with the help of the Arduino classes taught by Dr. Mundt, which are organized by the German Department. Students begins with basics and it is aimed them to apply the skills they learned by designing, building, and testing a microcontroller-based embedded system, producing creative projects.

IEL Robotics

The Robotics Club of our school grows by means of the classes given by the club members to junior paricipants, which cover Introduction to Arduino, C++ Libraries and their Utilaziton Introduction to Electronics and Making of Printed Circuits. The aim of he club classes is that new members reach the level of ability to help with on-going projects by the end of the school they join.

Computer Olimpiads

The preparitions for the national and international computer olimpiads cover mainly the classes Algorithm Design and Search Algorithms as well as Dynamic Programming, 2-Dimesional Linear Geometry, Data Structures and Time and Space Complexity. Our Olympiad team led by Semih Başarık has sent two students to this year's competition.

Photoshop Classes

Istanbul Lisesi vice principal Gürkan Sünnetçi, who is a computer teacher, teaches Photoshop classes to interested students. Students learn how to use tools, work with layers and masks.

Solidworks Classes

TSA Turkey Chapter organised technical education classes, where 3D-CAD was taught with the software called "Solidworks". Lessons were given by the president of TSA Chapter and included mathematical background knowledge, 3D-thinking and how to use Solidworks.

Classes by Science Comitee

Every january the Science Comitee organizes a science week. During this week a big variety of academicians visit our school and give conferences or classes about trend topics.

Medicine Programms

Interships in medical schools are organised for 11. graders by guidence service in Istanbul Lisesi. In these programms students have chances to see and experience what it likes to be a medic and a doctor.
