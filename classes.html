Classes and Courses

Arduino Classes

The students improve themselves in the areas of microcontrollers and embedded systems engineering with the help of the Arduino classes taught by Prof. Dr. Mundt, which are organized by the German Department.

IEL Robotics

The Robotics Club of our school grows bymeans of the classes given by the club members to junior paricipants, which cover Introduction to Arduino, C++ Libraries and their Utilaziton Introduction to Electronics and Making of Printed Circuits. The aimof he club classes is that new members reach the level of ability to help with on-going projects by the end of the school they join.
