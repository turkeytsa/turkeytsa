WHAT IS AUGMENTED REALITY?

Augmented reality is the ability to insert an overlay with digital and virtual information into the real world. Unlike virtual reality, which creates a totally artificial environment, augmented reality uses the existing environment and overlays new information on top of it. Augmented Reality has a lot of applications.

Technology

Augmented Reality can be seen through a wide variety of displays, from screens and monitors, to handheld devices or glasses.
Head mounted displays, like glasses and lenses, offers the widest visual field and provide more life-like Augmented Reality experiences.  Google Glass is one of the most common examples for head mounted displays. Unfortunately it has been stopped being produced because of privacy and safety concerns.(skip to safety problems) The next step up from glasses is contact lenses. It is aimed to eliminate the need for pesky headwear. They are still being developed, specifically for use in the military. (Skip to future uses)
Through mobile devices like smartphones and tablets, Augmented Reality act as a window. Through the viewer users can work with 3D models, view holograms or display information by using their camera.
Head-up display is also an augmented reality technology. A transparent display is positioned directly in the drivers’ or pilots’ view without requiring the user to look away from their usual viewpoints to get the data they need.

Potential Future Uses

Augmented reality has a huge potential to affect all industries, from advertising and marketing, to gaming and medicine.
In the future, we will be able to receive more efficient medical care. Augmented Reality applications will help surgeons during surgeries and we will have medical applications, which analyzes our state of health and gather our medical data. For example, CliniCloud, an Australian startup, made the world’s first stethoscope for home use. As technology continues to advance, we will see more medical applications. Also current technologies like True 3D wil develop and give us more accurate information every time.
Augmented reality has an important place in online-shopping either. For example, you could launch an augmented reality app that gives users a map of the store to help them find what they’re looking for or you could let users to visualize the product in real world, so that they could be sure before purchasing the product.
The next generation of augmented reality is Articulated Naturality Web (ANW) which is introduced by QderoPateo Communications . It is described as a "complete renaissance in the way we approach technology,” Information is going to be more integrated into the natural world rather than just being an overlay. We will be able to get a weather forecast just by holding our device up to sky. ANW would let us take a look inside the hotel room by looking at the outside of the hotel. It will be possible to try furniture in our office before buying them.
