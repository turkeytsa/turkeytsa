Projects at Istanbul Lisesi

Jugend Forscht

Jugendforscht is a competition sponsored by the Federal Ministry of Education and Research in Germany to support talented young people aged 15-21 in Mathematics, Programming, Natural Sciences and Technical fields. In the Jugend Forscht Science Competition held in Thuringia, Germany, Istanbul Lisesi students won 1 Gold and 4 Bronze medals. While the project titled "T-shirt blocking hunching" from the IEL Robotics Club was awarded a gold medal, the projects in the field of Biology were awarded with bronze medals.

Technology Student Association Achievements

Our team has participated in the competitions organized by the Technology Student Association since 2012. All teams and individual members have the opportunities to develop their skills during the school year in different areas. The large variety of events lets students get information about different fields, which helps them decide on their career more easily.
