Extracurricular Scientific Activities at Istanbul Lisesi

Computer Club
The Computer Club is instructed by computer and mathematics teacher Frank Meyer and takes place every Wednesday. Participation is voluntary and the club consists mostly of the students in preparatory class and ninth grade. In the first term participants learn how to create images with POV-Ray, a freeware ray tracing program. Below you can see some room visualizations prepared by the students. In the second term, students start working with HTML in order to create websites.
International Chess Festival
  
Being the first international chess festival for high school level in Turkey, the International Chess Festival is held every year since 2001. Nearly 60 schools from both Turkey and other countries take part in this festival which is arranged by the chess club of the school.
